Eleftherios (Lef) Ioannidis

PhD student in the University of Pennsylvania. I am broadly interested in formal proofs for distributed systems. More specifically in the denotational semantics of programming languages and modal logics for distributed systems, as well as zero-knowledge proofs and cryptographic compilers. I am coadvised by Sebastian Angel and Steve Zdancewic. Prior, I completed by Bachelor's and Masters in MIT EECS, my thesis advisors were Frans Kaashoek and Nickolai Zeldovich.
